Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom build take? +

2-4 weeks from technical plan approval to go-live. Simple projects (1-2 workflows): 2 weeks. Complex systems (5+ integrations): 4 weeks. We'll give you an exact timeline in your technical plan.

What if I need changes after the build? +

First 90 days: minor tweaks and optimizations included. Major scope changes: quoted separately. After 90 days: ongoing support available at $197/month or hourly rates.

Do I need technical knowledge? +

No. We handle all technical implementation. You just need to explain your workflow—we translate that into automation. Training included to ensure you're confident using the system.

What if my tools aren't supported? +

We integrate with 525+ apps. If your tool has an API, we can connect it. If not, we'll find workarounds (email parsing, webhooks, CSV imports). Feasibility confirmed in technical plan before you pay.

Can I see the technical plan before committing? +

Yes! The Voice AI consultation is FREE. We deliver a detailed technical plan within 48 hours—no payment required. Only pay if you approve the plan and want to move forward.

What's included in the 90-day support? +

Bug fixes, performance optimization, minor workflow adjustments, integration troubleshooting, and unlimited email support. Priority response time (under 24 hours).

Is there a money-back guarantee? +

30-day guarantee from go-live. If the system doesn't work as specified in the technical plan, we'll fix it or refund 100%. Your technical plan is our contract—we deliver what we promise.
