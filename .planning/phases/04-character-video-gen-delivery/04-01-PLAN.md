Transitions to `awaiting-composition` when all scenes done.
- `awaiting-composition` — sends "Scenes generated! Now composing your reveal video..." message. Stores scene URLs in `collectedData.sceneUrls`. Marks completed=true so the next module (composition+delivery in Plan 03's wiring) can pick up.

**Prompt construction** (5 scenes):
- Scene 0 (auto): Intro portrait — `"@{soraHandle} standing confidently in a professional studio setting, warm lighting, looking directly at camera, slight smile. {visualStyle} aesthetic."` — ShotType: `narrative`

**handleMessage**: In `intro` phase, any message triggers generation start. In `generating` phase, respond with "Your character video is being generated, please wait..." In `awaiting-composition` phase, respond with "Your reveal video is being composed..."
