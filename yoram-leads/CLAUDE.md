## Project Overview

This is a marketing and content strategy repository for Yoram Friedman Insurance Agency, focused on lead generation in the Israeli insurance market. The repository contains strategic analysis documents, content planning materials, and marketing blueprints rather than traditional code.

## Repository Structure

The repository contains markdown documents covering:
- Israeli insurance market competitive analysis and social media strategy
- Marketing campaign blueprints and content strategies
- Client intake processes and lead generation funnels
- Content creation guidelines for social media platforms (Facebook, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok)
- AI avatar implementation strategies for Hebrew content creation

- `Yoram Friedman Insurance Marketing and Content Strategy Blueprint.md` - Detailed client requirements and campaign specifications

## Content Focus Areas

- **Target Audience**: Israelis aged 40-55 with active mortgages, household income 10,000-15,000 NIS/month
- **Primary Services**: Life insurance, health insurance, mortgage insurance, critical illness coverage, pension planning
- **Lead Generation**: Free insurance portfolio reviews using government Har HaBituach system
- **Geographic Scope**: Israel-wide with focus on Haifa/Northern region
- **Language**: Hebrew content with professional, warm, family-oriented tone

## Regulatory Considerations

All content must comply with Israeli insurance advertising regulations:
- Agent identification and license number disclosure required
- Prohibition on unsubstantiated savings claims
- Cannot use "ייעוץ" (advisory) terminology without proper licensing
- All promotional content must be marked as advertising

## Content Creation Guidelines

- 80/20 rule: 80% educational content, 20% promotional
- Posting schedule: 3-4 times weekly on Facebook, 1 weekly YouTube video
- AI avatar implementation using HeyGen for Hebrew video content
- WhatsApp as primary lead communication channel
- Landing pages optimized for 3-field forms (name, phone, email)
