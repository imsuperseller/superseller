Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, AmEx), PayPal, and wire transfer for enterprise customers. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
Can I switch plans later? ▼
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
Do you offer refunds? ▼
Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all subscription plans. For custom solutions, we provide milestone-based payments with satisfaction guarantees at each stage.
What's included in support? ▼
All plans include email support with 24-hour response time. Custom Solutions and Lead Generation plans include priority support with dedicated account managers and faster response times.
Can I try before I buy? ▼
Yes! The Marketplace offers free preview templates, and Custom Solutions starts with a free voice AI consultation. Lead Generation includes a 7-day trial with 50 free leads.
Do you offer enterprise pricing? ▼
Yes! For teams of 10+ or annual contracts over $50,000, we offer custom enterprise pricing with volume discounts, dedicated support, and SLA guarantees. Contact sales@rensto.com for details.
How long does implementation take? ▼
Marketplace templates can be deployed in hours. Ready Solutions take 1-3 days. Custom Solutions typically take 2-6 weeks depending on complexity. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.
What if I need technical help? ▼
All plans include comprehensive documentation and video tutorials. Marketplace users get community support, while Custom and Lead Generation customers receive dedicated technical support from our team.
