# /competitor-brief — Competitor Intelligence Briefing

Research the competitive landscape and produce an actionable intelligence briefing for SuperSeller AI positioning.

## Inputs

- `competitors` (optional): Specific competitors to research. Defaults to the core set below.
- `focus` (optional): Specific angle -- "pricing", "features", "messaging", "new-launches", or "full". Defaults to "full".
- `timeframe` (optional): How recent. "this-week", "this-month", "quarter". Defaults to "this-month".

## Research Process

1. **Web search** each competitor for:
   - Latest pricing changes
   - New feature launches or announcements
   - AI-related updates (new models, new capabilities)
   - Customer reviews and complaints (G2, Capterra, Reddit, Twitter)
   - Messaging changes on their homepage/landing pages

2. **Analyze positioning**:
   - What problem do they lead with?
   - Who is their ICP (ideal customer profile)?
   - What is their primary CTA?
   - How do they price? (per seat, per feature, per usage)
   - What do their customers complain about most?

3. **Compare against SuperSeller AI**:
   - SuperSeller is the ONLY platform bundling all 7 AI functions (video, avatar, marketplace, voice, leads, social, intelligence)
   - Per-function pricing is 3-8x more expensive than specialists, but the bundle at $79/mo is the value play
   - Key gap: no free trial, limited social proof, no cost anchoring on website
   - Strengths: Israeli-American hustle culture brand, WhatsApp-first workflow, AI crew personification

## Output Format

### Executive Summary (3-5 bullets)
What changed this period. What matters. What to do about it.

### Threat Matrix

| Competitor | Threat Level | Recent Move | Our Response |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|--------------|
| ... | High/Med/Low | What they did | What we should do |

### Pricing Landscape
Table comparing pricing tiers across competitors vs SuperSeller.

### Messaging Analysis
How competitors are talking about AI -- what words, what promises, what proof points.

### Opportunity Gaps
Things competitors are NOT doing that SuperSeller can exploit:
- Bundled AI crew (no one else has named AI agents doing 7 functions)
- WhatsApp-native workflow (competitors are all email/web-first)
- Israeli/Jewish small biz community focus (untapped niche)
- Real estate vertical specialization (TourReel has no direct competitor at this price)

### Recommended Actions
3-5 specific, actionable recommendations with priority (P0/P1/P2).
