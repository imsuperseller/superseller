# Knowledge Base: Sales Offers - Audit & Sprint Planning

This document serves as the source of truth for Rensto's core entry-level service offers.

## 1. Automation Audit & Gap Analysis ($499)
**Goal**: Identify technical bottlenecks and manual overhead in existing operations.
**Outcome**: A "Custom Solution Blueprint" detailing where AI can save time and money.
**Guarantee**: "I guarantee you'll identify $25,000+ in annual savings within 2 hours, or I'll refund 100%."
**Process**:
- 60-90 minute deep-dive consultation.
- Full infrastructure review.
- High-level ROI projections.
- Priority roadmap for implementation.

## 2. Technical Sprint Planning ($1,500)
**Goal**: Deep technical architecture for a specific set of 3-5 high-impact workflows.
**Outcome**: Full technical specifications, n8n pipeline designs, and a 30-day execution plan.
**Guarantee**: "I guarantee you'll see ROI within 60 days, or I'll continue working for free until you do."
**Process**:
- 1:1 Engineering Session.
- Detailed step-by-step logic design for up to 5 workflows.
- Tool selection & integration mapping (CRM, ERP, Webhooks).
- Implementation timeline and resource allocation.

## 3. Product-to-Outcome Mapping
- If the customer is **confused** or **pre-revenue**: Direct them to the **Audit**.
- If the customer is **ready to build** but lacks the **engine**: Direct them to **Ready Solutions** (via Marketplace).
- If the customer has **complex operations** and needs a **bespoke system**: Direct them to **Sprint Planning**.
- If the customer wants a **fully managed WhatsApp Sales Agent**: Direct them to the **WhatsApp Operating System** page.
