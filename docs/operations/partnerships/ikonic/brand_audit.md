# Brand Audit: Ikonic Precision Styling Tools

This report consolidates the research data gathered during the investigation of **Ikonic Precision Styling Tools** and its owner, **Matt Kapulsky**.

## 1. Social Media Footprint & Engagement
The brand has a wide footprint but extremely low organic momentum.

| Platform | Follower Count | Engagement Level | Recency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Instagram** | ~2,130 | < 1% (Very Low) | Active but low interaction |
| **Facebook** | ~6,500 | ~0% (Dead) | Regular posts, zero likes/comments |
| **TikTok** | 11 | Non-existent | Inactive |
| **YouTube** | 191 | Low view counts | Recent "Shorts" have <100 views |

> [!WARNING]
> **Conclusion:** The brand lacks "Digital Heartbeat." There is no community or buzz. Any growth will require building a content engine from scratch.

## 2. Amazon Performance Analysis
*   **Revenue vs. Units:** Matt confirmed **140 units currently in stock**, with 500 more arriving in April.
*   **Return Rate:** Verified at **3%** (strong industry average), with targets to reach <1%.
*   **Fulfillment:** Moving to a professional logistics/shipping center (3PL) shortly.

## 3. Brand Reputation & Risk (Yellow Flags)
*   **Mall Kiosk History:** Associated with high-pressure sales and return refusal.
*   **Customer Support:** Weak response times reported; a major liability.
*   **Product Risk (Bristles):** I found recurring patterns of "bristle melting" and "hair pulling" on their blow-dryer brushes and stylers. These items carry a high return risk.
*   **Brand Identity (Verified):** The "IKONIC" trademark (Reg. No. 6196193) is **LIVE** and registered in the US for hair styling tools. 
*   **Legal Moat:** It is owned by Matt's corporate network (IVISION TECH, INC.). This confirms he has the exclusive legal right to sell and market these tools in the US, providing a heavy shield against the Indian brand confusion.
*   **Product Safety:** Verified no active CPSC or FDA recalls for the SuperNova line.
*   **Shipping Restrictions:** Contiguous 48 states only (No HI, AK, PR, or P.O. Boxes). Outreach must be filtered to these 48 states.

## 4. Owner: Matt Kapulsky
*   **Background:** Verified as the owner via LinkedIn. Based in Plano/Dallas.
*   **Network:** Relatively small LinkedIn network (~120 followers).
*   **Business Address:** Uses a virtual office/Regus space in Plano, TX.
