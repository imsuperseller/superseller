# 💰 Rensto Productization Guide: "Selling the Methods"

This guide explains how to turn our internal n8n "Gems" into high-ticket products by positioning them as **Methods** within the 4 Pillars.

## 💎 The Psychology of "Methods"
Instead of selling a "Workflow" (technical), we sell a **"Method"** (proprietary result). This justifies higher prices and prevents clients from trying to "do it themselves".

### Example: The "COO Growth Method"
- **Technical**: `conti-growth-agent-v2.json`
- **Product**: A specialized AI agent that monitors every lead 24/7 and follow-ups with CEO-level professionalism.
- **Value**: Replaces a part-time assistant ($1,500/mo).
- **Pricing**: $497 add-on or included in the $5,497 Bundle.

- **Lead Sourcing?** -> Pillar 1 (Lead Machine)
- **Voice/Chat/Secretary?** -> Pillar 2 (Autonomous Secretary)
