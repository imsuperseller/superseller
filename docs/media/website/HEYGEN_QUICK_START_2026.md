## ⚡ **QUICK START: CREATE YOUR FIRST VIDEO IN 15 MINUTES**

### **Step 1: Access HeyGen Video Agent**
1. Go to https://heygen.com
2. Login to your account
3. Click "Create Video" → "Video Agent"
4. Select "Generate from Prompt"

### **Step 2: Use Optimized Prompt Template**

Copy this template and fill in your details:

```
VIDEO TYPE: [Hero Background / Service Card / Demo / Testimonial]
DURATION: [15s / 30s / 60s / 90s / 2min]
BRAND: SuperSeller AI (Brand Shuffle - colors: #fe3d51, #1eaef7, #bf5700, #5ffbfd on #110d28 dark background)
AVATAR: Professional business avatar
STYLE: Modern, professional, empathetic rebel tone

SCRIPT:
[Your complete script here - 150-200 words for 60s video]

VISUAL REQUIREMENTS:
- Background: [Describe what you want - e.g., "Split-screen showing manual chaos vs automated control"]
- Graphics: [List graphics needed - e.g., "Show Gmail inbox, Google Sheets, WhatsApp icons"]
- Animations: [Describe animations - e.g., "Transition from chaos to organized dashboard"]
- Logo Placement: Start (2s) and end (2s)
- Screenshots/Media: [List any specific screenshots to include]

MOOD/TONE:
- Problem-focused opening
- Empathetic but direct
- Clear value proposition
- Strong CTA

BRAND ELEMENTS:
- Colors: Primary #fe3d51, Secondary #1eaef7, Accent #bf5700, Highlight #5ffbfd
- Background: Dark theme #110d28
- Logo: SuperSeller AI logo at start and end
```

### **Step 3: Generate Video**
1. Paste your optimized prompt
2. Select "SuperSeller AI" brand (Brand Shuffle auto-applies)
3. Click "Generate Video"
4. Wait 2-5 minutes

### **Step 4: Review & Export**
1. Review generated video
2. Make minor adjustments if needed (HeyGen handles most automatically)
3. Export 1080p
4. Upload to YouTube (unlisted)
5. Get video ID for integration
