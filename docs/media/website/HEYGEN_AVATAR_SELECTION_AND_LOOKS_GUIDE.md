## 🎯 **AVATAR SELECTION RULES**

### **✅ USE @SHAI (Yourself)** When:

1. **Founder/Personal Videos**:
   - Testimonials (Shelly, Tax4Us testimonials)
   - Personal stories ("Sheets to Airtable Migration Story")
   - Process explanations where you're explaining YOUR process
   - Consultation demos where you're the consultant
   - Founder authenticity needed

2. **High-Trust Content**:
   - Custom Solutions consultation demo
   - "How It Works" process explanations
   - Case study testimonials
   - Personal transformation stories

3. **First-Person Narratives**:
   - Videos where script uses "I" or "we" (SuperSeller AI team)
   - Behind-the-scenes content
   - Real customer success stories

### **❌ DON'T USE @SHAI** When:

1. **Generic Service Videos**:
   - Hero background videos (generic business owner)
   - Service card videos (representative customer)
   - Industry-specific videos (industry specialist needed)

2. **Character-Based Content**:
   - "Tool Hoarder" character
   - "Follow-Up Failure" character
   - "Weekend Warrior" character
   - "Heroic Martyr" character

3. **Industry-Specific Videos**:
   - HVAC specialist (use HVAC professional avatar)
   - Realtor specialist (use realtor avatar)
   - Dentist specialist (use dentist avatar)
   - etc.

## 🛠️ **HOW TO CREATE AVATAR LOOKS IN HEYGEN**

### **For @shai Looks**:

1. **Open HeyGen** → Go to "Avatars" section
2. **Select @shai** avatar
3. **Click "Avatar Looks"** or "Create Look"
4. **Enter Look Name**: e.g., "Professional Consultant"
5. **Paste Look Prompt** from above (e.g., Professional Consultant Look prompt)
6. **Customize**:
   - Outfit selection
   - Background selection
   - Lighting adjustment
7. **Save Look** with descriptive name
8. **Use in Video**: When creating video, select @shai → Choose saved look

### **For Professional Business Avatar Looks**:

1. **Open HeyGen** → Go to "Avatars" section
2. **Select "Professional Business Avatar"** (or create new)
3. **Click "Avatar Looks"** or "Create Look"
4. **Enter Look Name**: e.g., "Generic Business Owner"
5. **Paste Look Prompt** from above
6. **Customize** and **Save**
7. **Use in Video**: Select avatar → Choose saved look

### **For Industry Specialist Avatar Looks**:

1. **Open HeyGen** → Go to "Avatars" section
2. **Select "Industry Specialist Avatar"** (or create new)
3. **Click "Avatar Looks"** or "Create Look"
4. **Enter Look Name**: e.g., "HVAC Professional"
5. **Paste Industry-Specific Look Prompt** from above
6. **Customize** and **Save**
7. **Use in Video**: Select avatar → Choose saved look
