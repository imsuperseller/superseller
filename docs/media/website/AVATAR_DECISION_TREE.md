## 🎯 **QUICK DECISION TREE**

### **Question 1: Is this a personal/founder video?**

**YES** → Use **@shai** with appropriate look:
- ✅ Testimonials (Shelly, Tax4Us)
- ✅ Consultation demos (you're the consultant)
- ✅ Process explanations (you're explaining YOUR process)
- ✅ Personal stories ("Sheets to Airtable Migration Story")

**NO** → Go to Question 2

---

### **Question 2: Is this an industry-specific video?**

**YES** → Use **Industry Specialist Avatar**:
- ✅ HVAC, Realtor, Roofer, Dentist, etc. (16 industry videos)
- ✅ Use industry-specific look (e.g., "HVAC Professional Look")

**NO** → Go to Question 3

---

### **Question 3: Is this a character-based video?**

**YES** → Use **Professional Business Avatar** with character look:
- ✅ "Tool Hoarder" character (Marketplace service card)
- ✅ "Follow-Up Failure" character (Subscriptions service card)
- ✅ "Optimistic Multi-Tasker" character (Ready Solutions service card)
- ✅ "Heroic Martyr" character (Custom Solutions service card)
- ✅ "Weekend Warrior" character (Pain point section)

**NO** → Use **Professional Business Avatar** with Generic Business Owner look:
- ✅ Hero background videos (all pages)
- ✅ Generic service videos

## 🎨 **AVATAR LOOK PROMPTS**

### **For @shai**:

**Professional Consultant Look**:
```
Professional business consultant, dark navy suit or dark blazer, white dress shirt, modern office background with subtle SuperSeller AI brand colors (#fe3d51, #1eaef7) visible, professional studio lighting, warm tone, authoritative but approachable, trustworthy expert appearance, confident but empathetic expression, modern professional aesthetic
```

**Founder/Personal Look**:
```
Founder personal appearance, casual professional, branded t-shirt or dark shirt with SuperSeller AI logo visible, home office background or modern workspace, warm natural lighting, authentic personal appearance, relatable founder vibe, genuine expression, approachable and authentic
```

### **For Professional Business Avatar**:

**Generic Business Owner Look**:
```
Generic business owner, professional appearance, modern business casual or professional attire, neutral modern office background, professional lighting, relatable business owner appearance, represents target customer, approachable professional, confident but slightly overwhelmed expression (for problem-focused videos) or calm organized expression (for solution-focused videos)
```
