# 🤍 WHITE-GLOVE CLIENT EXPERIENCE FRAMEWORK

*Creating the "Burberry Experience" for Rensto Clients*

## 🎯 **THE PSYCHOLOGY: Why Experience Matters**

### **The Burberry Model Applied to Rensto**

**Burberry Strategy:**
- Limited access (lineup outside store)
- Personal service (dedicated style specialist)
- Complimentary services (free measurements, drinks)
- Premium environment (beautiful store, amazing people)
- **Result:** $500 for a $40 shirt

**Rensto Application:**
- Limited availability (only 3 clients per month)
- Personal service (dedicated automation strategist)
- Complimentary services (free audits, consultations)
- Premium deliverables (custom branding, video updates)
- **Result:** 5-10x higher pricing justified

**✅ NEW:** "I'm currently working with 3 strategic partners this month, but I have 1 slot available for a business owner who's serious about **doubling their automation ROI**."

## 🎥 **VIDEO UPDATE SYSTEM**

### **The "Facetime Without Sunk Cost" Strategy**

**Why Videos Work:**
- Creates illusion of personal attention
- Builds trust and connection
- Demonstrates expertise
- **Cost:** 5 minutes vs 1 hour call

### **Video Update Template:**

```
"Hey [Client Name],

It's [Your Name] from Rensto. I wanted to give you a quick update on your automation project.

✅ **What's Done:**
- [Specific accomplishment]
- [Specific accomplishment]

🚀 **What's Next:**
- [Next milestone]
- [Expected timeline]

💡 **Strategic Insight:**
- [Business insight beyond scope]

I'm excited about the results we're going to achieve together. Talk to you Wednesday!

Best,
[Your Name]"
```

**Implementation:**
- [ ] Record 2-3 minute videos
- [ ] Use Loom or similar tool
- [ ] Send via email with personal message
- [ ] Include in project management system
