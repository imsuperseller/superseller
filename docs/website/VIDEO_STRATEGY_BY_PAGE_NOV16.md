## 📝 **SCRIPT TEMPLATES**

### **Hero Video Template** (30-60 seconds)

**Structure**:
1. **Hook** (0:00-0:10): Problem statement
2. **Problem** (0:10-0:25): Pain points, consequences
3. **Solution** (0:25-0:40): How Rensto solves it
4. **Benefits** (0:40-0:50): Key benefits
5. **CTA** (0:50-0:60): Pricing + next step

**Example - Custom Solutions Hero**:
```
[HOOK] You're working 50+ hour weeks. Gmail chaos. Google Sheets scattered. WhatsApp unanswered. [PROBLEM] Manual work is killing your business. Your competitor is ahead because they automated. [SOLUTION] Custom automation built for YOUR business. We connect YOUR tools. Gmail. WhatsApp. Airtable. OpenAI. Claude. Gemini. [BENEFITS] Agent handles YOUR busywork. You focus on growth. [CTA] Free consultation. $3,500 to $8,000. Get started today.
```

---

### **Demo Video Template** (60-90 seconds)

**Structure**:
1. **Hook** (0:00-0:10): What you'll see
2. **Step 1** (0:10-0:25): First step explanation
3. **Step 2** (0:25-0:40): Second step explanation
4. **Step 3** (0:40-0:55): Third step explanation
5. **Results** (0:55-1:05): Benefits/results
6. **CTA** (1:05-1:15): Next step

## 📋 **CREATION WORKFLOW**

### **For Each Video**:

1. **Write Script** (using templates above)
2. **Open HeyGen UI** → Create Video
3. **Select Avatar** (professional business avatar)
4. **Paste Script**
5. **Add Media**:
   - Rensto logo (start/end)
   - Screenshots (if needed for demo videos)
6. **Generate** → Wait 2-5 minutes
7. **Export** 1080p
8. **Upload to YouTube** (unlisted)
9. **Get Video ID**
10. **Integrate into page** (see integration guide)
11. **Deploy to production**
