```
├── /legal/
│   ├── /privacy (Privacy Policy)
│   └── /terms (Terms of Service)
├── /app/ (Customer Portal)
│   ├── /dashboard
│   ├── /templates
│   ├── /agents
│   ├── /billing
│   ├── /settings
│   └── /support
└── /admin/ (Admin Dashboard)
    ├── /customers
    ├── /revenue
    ├── /system
    └── /workflows
```

### **URL Structure**
- **Clean URLs**: `/pricing` not `/pricing-page`
- **Descriptive URLs**: `/features/universal-templates` not `/features/1`
- **Consistent Structure**: All pages follow same pattern
- **No Trailing Slashes**: `/pricing` not `/pricing/`

### **Internal Linking Strategy**
