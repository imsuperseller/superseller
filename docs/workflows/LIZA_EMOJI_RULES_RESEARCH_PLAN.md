## 🎯 **PROPOSED EMOJI RULES** (Draft - Needs Validation)

### **✅ APPROPRIATE EMOJIS**

**Professional Context**:
- ✅ **Product/Technical**: 📐 (ruler), 🏠 (house), 🎨 (art), 📏 (measurements)
- ✅ **Showrooms**: 📍 (location), 🗺️ (map), 🚗 (car)
- ✅ **Collections**: ✨ (sparkle), 💎 (gem), 🌿 (nature)
- ✅ **Confirmation**: ✅ (checkmark), 👍 (thumbs up)
- ✅ **Information**: ℹ️ (info), 📋 (clipboard), 📞 (phone)

**Friendly Context**:
- ✅ **Greetings**: 👋 (wave), 😊 (smile)
- ✅ **Positive**: 😃 (happy), 🎉 (celebration)
- ✅ **Helpful**: 💡 (lightbulb), 🤝 (handshake)

### **❌ INAPPROPRIATE EMOJIS**

**Too Casual**:
- ❌ 😂 (laughing), 🤣 (rolling), 😜 (winking)
- ❌ 💯 (100), 🔥 (fire), 💪 (muscle)
- ❌ ❤️ (heart), 💕 (hearts), 😍 (heart eyes)

**Unprofessional**:
- ❌ 🍕 (pizza), 🍔 (burger), 🍺 (beer)
- ❌ 🎮 (gaming), 🎬 (movie), 🎵 (music)
- ❌ 💸 (money), 💰 (money bag), 🤑 (money face)
