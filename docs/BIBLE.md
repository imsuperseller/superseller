## 🌟 Vision & Core Psychology
**"Build Faster with Pre-Built Tools"**

Rensto operates on the belief that businesses shouldn't reinvent the wheel. We resell high-leverage "modkits" and "engines" that plug into existing operations.

### Key Psychological Hooks
- **"The Ecosystem"**: We don't sell tools; we sell a complete ecosystem (The 4 Pillars).
- **"Engine" Metaphor**: Products are "Engines" (Lead Machine, Content Engine, etc.) that drive specific business outcomes.
- **"Modkits"**: Smaller, modular capabilities that enhance existing engines.
- **"Audit First"**: We sell trust through the "Automation Audit" ($497) which diagnoses issues before prescribing expensive solutions.

---

## ⚙️ The Product Ecosystem (The 4 Pillars)

1.  **Lead Machine**: Autonomous sourcing, enrichment, and outreach.
2.  **Autonomous Secretary**: Handling inbound comms, booking, and scheduling.
3.  **Knowledge Engine**: RAG systems, internal wikis, and expert bots.
4.  **Content Engine**: Creation, distribution, and social growth.
