# Rensto WhatsApp Agent: "The Zen of Automation"
**Concept**: A satirical yet ultra-premium visual journey contrasting "Old Chaos" with "Rensto Order".
**Total Duration**: 25 Seconds (5 Scenes x 5s)

---

## Storyboard & Prompts

### SCENE 1: The Chaos (0:00 - 0:05)
**Metric**: Anxiety, Overload, Clutter
**Visual**: A high-end office desk buried under oscillating phones.
**Sora 2 Prompt**:
> Use a cinematic macro lens. A sleek mahogany executive desk is completely submerged under a vibrating mountain of iPhones, each displaying different WhatsApp notifications like "Where is my order?", "Price??", "Hello?". The room is dimly lit with cold, stressful blue light. The camera pushes in slowly through the chaos of glowing screens. Hyper-realistic, 8k, slight dust particles dancing in the notification light. Smoke gently rising from one overheated phone. Satirical undertone of modern business overwhelm.

### SCENE 2: The Purification (0:05 - 0:10)
**Metric**: Transformation, Speed, Cleaning
**Visual**: A digital "wind" sweeping the phones away, leaving a single device.
**Sora 2 Prompt**:
> Wide angle, slow motion. A shockwave of emerald-green digital energy sweeps across the messy desk, instantly vaporizing the mountain of phones into organized streams of binary code. The clutter vanishes. What remains is a single, floating, pristine glass smartphone in the center of the air. The lighting shifts from stressful blue to a warm, confident golden hour sunbeam striking the phone. Minimalist, zen aesthetic.

### SCENE 3: The Engine (0:10 - 0:15)
**Metric**: Complexity made Simple, Logic, AI
**Visual**: Inside the phone—a beautiful, clockwork digital brain.
**Sora 2 Prompt**:
> Use a probe lens entering the glass of the single smartphone. Inside provides a view of a vast, infinite white space where glowing green geometric blocks are self-assembling into a perfect tower. Each block represents a conversation being handled. It looks like a high-tech satisfying ASMR video. "Inbound" blocks slide perfectly into "Fulfillment" slots without friction. The architecture is impossibly clean, like a Swiss watch made of light.

### SCENE 4: The Result (0:15 - 0:20)
**Metric**: Profit, Calm, Automations
**Visual**: The business owner sipping espresso while the phone works itself.
**Sora 2 Prompt**:
> Medium shot. A stylish business owner in a quiet, hyper-modern concrete loft, sitting in an Eames chair. They are holding a tiny espresso cup, looking out a window with a serene smile. In the blurred foreground, the single phone rests on a table, screen racing with "Payment Received" notifications, scrolling so fast it's just a green blur. The contrast between the owner's stillness and the phone's velocity is the satire. High fashion photography style.

### SCENE 5: The Rensto Seal (0:20 - 0:25)
**Metric**: Authority, Brand
**Visual**: The Rensto Logo forming from green smoke.
**Sora 2 Prompt**:
> A moody, dark void. Emerald green smoke swirls and condenses to form the sharp, metallic letters of "RENSTO". A subtitle fades in below in elegant sans-serif font: "Operating System for the 1%". The smoke dissipates leaving the logo gleaming in a spotlight. 8k, unreal engine 5 render style, cinematic ending.
