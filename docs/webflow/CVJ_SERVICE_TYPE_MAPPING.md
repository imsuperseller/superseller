## 📊 THE 4 SERVICE TYPES & THEIR AUDIENCES

### **1. Ready Solutions (Niche-Specific Packages)**
**Audience**: Industry operators who want proven, plug-and-play automation
**Pain**: "I need solutions NOW for my specific industry"
**Intent**: High urgency, low trust (first-time buyer)
**Journey**: Engage → Subscribe → Convert

### **2. Custom Solutions (Bespoke Automation)**
**Audience**: Business owners with unique workflows needing tailored systems
**Pain**: "Off-the-shelf doesn't fit my business"
**Intent**: High value, high consideration, needs consultation
**Journey**: Engage → Subscribe → Convert → Excite

### **3. Subscriptions (Lead Generation Service)**
**Audience**: Sales-driven businesses needing constant lead flow
**Pain**: "I need qualified leads every month"
**Intent**: Recurring value, metrics-focused, ROI-driven
**Journey**: Engage → Subscribe → Convert → Ascend

### **4. Marketplace (DIY Templates)**
**Audience**: Tech-savvy users who want to implement themselves
**Pain**: "I can do it myself if I have the right template"
**Intent**: Low cost, self-serve, fast implementation
**Journey**: Engage → Convert → Excite (skip Subscribe for quick buyers)

## 🔄 CROSS-SERVICE ROUTING

### **Ascension Paths (Upsell Flow)**
- Marketplace buyer → Full-Service Install → Ready Solutions
- Ready Solutions buyer → Add more solutions → Custom build
- Subscriptions user → CRM automation → Custom Solutions
- Custom Solutions client → Lead gen add-on → Subscriptions

### **Descent Paths (Downsell/Nurture)**
- Custom consultation shopper → Download scorecard → Nurture → Ready Solutions
- Ready Solutions browser → Download checklist → Nurture → Marketplace template
- Subscriptions browser → Free sample leads → Nurture → Low-tier plan
