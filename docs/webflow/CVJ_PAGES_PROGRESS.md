## 🎯 KEY CVJ PRINCIPLES APPLIED

### **1. Audience-First Messaging**
Every page starts with "Who it's for" in the hero:
- Ready Solutions: "HVAC/Roofer/Realtor? Get industry-proven..."
- Custom Solutions: "Your business is unique..."
- Subscriptions: "Sales-driven businesses..."
- Marketplace: "Tech-savvy users who..."

### **2. Problem → Mechanism → Outcome**
All pages use Deiss's 3-block pattern:
- **Problem**: Why previous attempts failed
- **Mechanism**: Our unique approach
- **Outcome**: Specific end state + timeframe

### **3. Journey-Aligned CTAs**
No "buy now" CTAs on Engage pages:
- Engage: "Browse Industries" / "See Projects"
- Subscribe: "Download Checklist" / "Get Scorecard"
- Convert: "Get Package" / "Book Consultation"
- Ascend: "Upgrade Plan" / "Add Full-Service"

### **4. Proof Early and Often**
Each viewport pairs claims with evidence:
- Stats bars (500+ businesses, 45+ projects, etc.)
- Case studies with metrics (not testimonials)
- ROI numbers in every section
- Review counts and ratings

### **5. Risk Reversal**
All pages remove purchase friction:
- 30-day money-back guarantee (no questions)
- FREE consultations / samples / templates
- No credit card required for trials
- Priority support included

### **6. Objection-Ordered FAQs**
FAQs address blockers in priority order:
1. Implementation time
2. Technical knowledge required
3. Integration compatibility
4. Pricing/refunds
5. Support duration
