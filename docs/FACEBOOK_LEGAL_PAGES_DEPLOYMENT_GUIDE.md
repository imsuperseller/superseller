## 🚀 **IMMEDIATE DEPLOYMENT (2 MINUTES)**

### **Option 1: Netlify (Recommended - Fastest)**
1. **Go to**: https://netlify.com
2. **Sign up/Login** with your account
3. **Drag and drop** the `netlify-deploy` folder from your computer
4. **Wait 30 seconds** for deployment
5. **Copy the URL** (e.g., `https://amazing-site-123456.netlify.app`)

### **Option 2: GitHub Pages**
1. **Create a new GitHub repository**
2. **Upload** the `netlify-deploy` folder contents
3. **Enable GitHub Pages** in repository settings
4. **Use the GitHub Pages URL**

2. **Import** the `netlify-deploy` folder
3. **Deploy** and get the URL

## 🎯 **FACEBOOK APPROVAL PROCESS**

### **Step 1: Deploy Legal Pages**
- Use any of the deployment options above
- Ensure pages are publicly accessible

### **Step 2: Submit to Facebook**
1. **Go to**: Facebook Business Manager
2. **Navigate to**: Business Settings > Legal
3. **Add URLs**:
   - Privacy Policy: `https://your-deployment-url/privacy-policy`
   - Terms of Service: `https://your-deployment-url/terms-of-service`
4. **Submit for review**

### **Step 3: Wait for Approval**
- Facebook typically responds within 24-48 hours
- You'll receive email notification of approval
