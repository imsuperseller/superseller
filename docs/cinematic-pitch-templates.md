# Cinematic Ego-Pitch Templates

This document defines the creative templates for the engine. Each template includes the visual prompts for Sora (or similar video AI) and the script structure for HeyGen (or similar TTS).

## Template 1: "The Temple of [Prospect]" (God-tier POV)

**Concept:** First-person POV. The prospect *is* the deity. The camera looks out/down from a high throne.
**Tone:** Epic, reverent, immersive.

### Visuals (Sora Prompts)

| Scene | Duration | Sora Prompt Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. The View** | 5s | First-person POV from a high marble throne looking down at a vast, futuristic temple hall. Clouds drift near the floor. A massive banner hangs with the text "{{prospect.company}}". |
| **2. The Chaos** | 5s | POV looking down at a group of clumsy, gray, glitching robots (labeled "Current Process") dropping papers and running into each other. |
| **3. The Arrival** | 5s | The gray robots are swept away by a wave of light. A sleek, confident figure (Rensto Avatar / @shai-lfc) walks forward through the light, looking up directly at the camera. |
| **4. The Offering** | 5s | The figure (@shai-lfc) holds up a glowing holographic tablet. The camera zooms in on the tablet showing "REVENUE UP" and "{{business.offer}}". |
| **5. Outro** | 5s | The figure bows respectfully, then points at a "YES" button floating in the air in front of the camera. Text overlay: "COMMAND YOUR EMPIRE." |

### Script (HeyGen Voiceover)

**Voice:** Deep, authoritative narrator (internal monologue or servant's voice).

> "This is your view, **{{prospect.name}}**.
>
> From your throne at **{{prospect.company}}**, you see the chaos of **{{prospect.pain_points[0]}}**.
>
> It is beneath you.
>
> We have brought order. A **{{business.offer}}** system, built for a king.
>
> It turns **{{prospect.pain_points[1]}}** into profit.
>
> Your command, **{{prospect.role}}**?"

## Template 2: "Shark Tank" (The Shark's Eyes)

**Concept:** First-person POV. The prospect is the Shark in the chair.
**Tone:** High-stakes, reality TV, direct.

### Visuals (Sora Prompts)

| Scene | Duration | Sora Prompt Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. The Chair** | 5s | First-person POV sitting in a leather chair. You see your own hands (wearing a generic expensive watch) resting on the armrests. In front, a nervous, sweaty person in a cheap suit (labeled "Manual Work") is fumbling with papers. |
| **2. The Rejection** | 5s | POV: You (the camera) press a big red buzzer. The nervous person looks devastated and walks away. |
| **3. The Pitch** | 5s | A confident entrepreneur (@shai-lfc) steps onto the carpet, smiling, looking directly into the camera lens. He is holding a golden ticket. |
| **4. The Deal** | 5s | Close up on @shai-lfc's face as he presents the ticket. The ticket reads: "INVEST: {{business.pricing_tiers[0].price}} -> RETURN: {{business.case_studies[0].result}}". |
| **5. Outro** | 5s | @shai-lfc extends his hand towards the camera for a handshake. Text overlay: "DO WE HAVE A DEAL?" |

### Script (HeyGen Voiceover)

**Voice:** Sharp, energetic narrator.

> "You are the Shark. The money.
>
> That mess you just fired? That was **{{prospect.pain_points[0]}}**. Bad investment.
>
> I'm here to pitch the unicorn.
>
> We install **{{business.offer}}** for **{{prospect.company}}**.
>
> You put in **{{business.pricing_tiers[0].price}}**. You get back **{{business.case_studies[0].result}}**.
>
> The other sharks are begging for this deal.
>
> But I'm offering it to you, **{{prospect.name}}**. Do we have a deal?"
