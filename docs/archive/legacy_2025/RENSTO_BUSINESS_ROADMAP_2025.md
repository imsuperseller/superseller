## 📊 **BUSINESS MODEL OVERVIEW**

### **💰 REVENUE MODEL**
**Primary**: Subscription-based with usage billing
- **Marketplace**: n8n templates & installation services
- **Custom Solutions**: Voice AI consultation & tailored automation
- **Subscriptions**: Enhanced hot leads service with CRM integration
- **WhatsApp Agent**: Modular 24/7 operating system (Base + Add-ons)
- **Ready Solutions**: Niche-specific automation packages

**Secondary**: Usage-based billing for high-volume customers
- **API Calls**: $0.01 per call
- **Data Processing**: $0.10 per GB
- **Custom Integrations**: $500-$2000 per integration

### **🎯 TARGET MARKET**
**Primary**: Small to medium businesses (10-500 employees)
- **Industries**: Technology, Healthcare, Finance, Education, Retail
- **Pain Points**: Manual processes, data silos, lack of automation
- **Budget**: $100-$1000/month for automation solutions

**Secondary**: Enterprise customers (500+ employees)
- **Industries**: Fortune 500 companies, government agencies
- **Pain Points**: Complex workflows, compliance requirements
- **Budget**: $1000-$10000/month for enterprise solutions
