**Webflow Designer**:

1. **Open Webflow Designer**

   - Open site: Rensto
   - Navigate to **Marketplace** page

2. **Find the Page Content Section**
   - Look for the middle section between Nav and Footer
   - (This is where workflow cards should appear)

3. **Add Div Block**
   - Click **Add** (+) button
   - Select **Div Block**
   - OR: Press `D` key for quick add

4. **Set Class**
   - Click the div element
   - In **Element Settings** → **Settings** tab
   - Add class: `workflows-container`
   - Press Enter

5. **Publish**
   - Click **Publish** button (top right)
   - Select domains to publish
   - Click **Publish**
