📋 Deployment Instructions

  1. First time? Open Webflow Dashboard in a new tab and log in
  2. Find and click on "Rensto" site in your dashboard
  3. Click "Open Designer" button (blue button, top right)
  4. In Designer: Click "Pages" (left sidebar) → Select the page you're working on
  5. Click the gear icon (Page Settings)
  6. Scroll to "Custom Code" section
  7. Click "Copy Code" button below, paste in "Before </body> tag" field
  8. Click "Save" → Click "Publish" (top right)
  9. Come back here and click "Mark as Done"

💡 Pro Tip: Keep Webflow Designer open in one tab and this helper in another. Copy the code once, then paste it into each page without switching tabs repeatedly.
