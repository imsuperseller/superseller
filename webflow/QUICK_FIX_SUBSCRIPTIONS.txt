🔧 QUICK FIX: Subscriptions Checkout (5 MINUTES)

============================================================
STEP 1: Open Webflow Designer
- Go to webflow.com/designer
- Open Rensto site

STEP 2: Navigate to Subscriptions Page
- Click Pages in sidebar
- Find "Subscriptions" page
- Open it

STEP 3: Open Page Settings
- Click gear icon ⚙️ in top toolbar
- Page Settings panel opens (right side)

STEP 4: Find Custom Code
- Scroll down to "Custom Code" section
- Find "Code before </body> tag" field

STEP 5: Copy & Paste This Code:
<script src="https://rensto-webflow-scripts.vercel.app/shared/stripe-core.js"></script>
<script src="https://rensto-webflow-scripts.vercel.app/subscriptions/checkout.js?v=2"></script>

STEP 6: Save & Publish
- Click Save in Page Settings
- Click Publish in top toolbar
- Select rensto.com and www.rensto.com
- Publish!

✅ DONE! Checkout buttons work immediately.
