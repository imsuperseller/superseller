## Group Profile

A mutual-aid community group for Israeli/Jewish diaspora in DFW (primarily Plano, Frisco, Richardson, North Dallas). NOT a business group — it's a community group where people ask for help, recommend services, discuss Israel politics, share food, and support each other.

**Political lean**: Strongly right-wing, pro-Israel, pro-Trump.
**Religious**: Diverse (secular to orthodox). Debates get heated but tolerance exists.
**Tone**: Warm, helpful, direct. Emergency requests get immediate response. Spam gets admin-deleted.

## Posting Rules

1. **Hebrew first** — the group is 80% Hebrew
2. **Short** — under 10 lines. No walls of text.
3. **Value first** — solve a problem, don't pitch
4. **Visual proof** — food/video posts get looked at even if people complain
5. **DM call-to-action** — say "send me a message", not a link
6. **Never post outside Sunday** — admin Amit Galor WILL delete and publicly call you out
7. **Be Shai from the group** — you're a known member, not a stranger. Leverage that.
8. **Rotate formats** — same ad every week = fatigue (see Meat Point/Tokyo Bar backlash)
