## 2. Content That Works for Contractor Social Media (2026)

### Format Performance (Instagram 2026 Benchmarks)

| Format | Avg Engagement Rate | Best Use for Contractors |
|---|---|---|
| **Carousels** | **0.55%** (highest) | Before/after reveals (swipe to see transformation) |
| **Reels** | 0.52% | Time-lapses, day-in-the-life, quick tips |
| **Static Photos** | Lower | Completed project hero shots |

**Important**: Carousels outperform Reels for engagement in 2026. The "swipe to reveal" before/after format is perfect for remodeling.

### Proven Content Types for Contractors

**Tier 1 — Highest Engagement**
- **Before/After Carousels**: Swipe-to-reveal transformations. Kitchen and bathroom remodels get the most saves and shares.
- **Time-lapse Reels**: Full project compressed to 15-30 seconds. Demolition-to-completion format goes viral on TikTok/Reels.
- **"The Reveal" Videos**: Homeowner reaction when they see the finished project. Emotional content gets shared.

**Tier 2 — Authority Building**
- **Process/Educational Content**: "3 things your contractor won't tell you about bathroom remodels." Quick tips build trust and get saved.
- **Material Selection Tips**: Countertop comparisons, flooring durability tests, paint finish explanations.
- **Behind-the-Scenes**: Crew at work, problem-solving on the job. Authenticity > polish on social.

**Tier 3 — Trust & Social Proof**
- **Customer Testimonials**: Screenshot testimonials, video testimonials, Google review highlights.
- **Team/Crew Content**: Introduce the team, celebrate milestones. Humanizes the brand.
- **Community/Local Content**: DFW-specific seasonal tips (Texas heat prep, storm damage, etc.).

### Posting Frequency (Recommended for GP Homes)

| Platform | Recommended Frequency | Content Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **Instagram Feed** | 3-5x per week | Project photos, carousels, testimonials |
| **Instagram Stories** | Daily | Behind-the-scenes, polls, job updates |
| **Instagram Reels** | 3-4x per week | Time-lapses, tips, reveals |
| **Facebook** | 2x per week | Project showcases, community engagement |
| **TikTok** | 2-3x per week | Time-lapses, before/after, educational |

### Content Calendar Framework (Monthly)

| Week | Monday | Wednesday | Friday |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | Before/after carousel | Educational reel (tip) | Testimonial/review |
| **2** | Project showcase photo | Time-lapse reel | Team/behind-scenes |
| **3** | Before/after carousel | Material comparison reel | Customer story |
| **4** | Seasonal/local tip | Process walkthrough reel | Completed project showcase |

Plus daily Stories (job site updates, polls, material shopping, etc.)

## 3. Social Media Management Pricing (Market Rates 2026)

### What Agencies Charge

| Tier | Monthly Cost | What's Included |
|---|---|---|
| **Basic / Starter** | $500 - $1,500/mo | 10-20 posts/month, 1-2 platforms, basic community management, monthly reporting |
| **Standard / Mid-Tier** | $1,500 - $4,000/mo | 20-40 posts/month, 2-4 platforms, custom graphics + videos, weekly community management, bi-weekly strategy calls |
| **Premium / Full-Service** | $4,000 - $10,000/mo | 30+ posts/month, all platforms, video production, ad management, influencer outreach, advanced analytics |
| **Enterprise (WebFX etc.)** | $3,150 - $5,100+/mo | 10-30 posts, custom images, dedicated account manager, strategy development, boosted posts, 750+ expert team |

### Pricing by Provider Type

| Provider | Typical Range | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Freelancer** | $500 - $2,000/mo | Affordable, personal attention | Quality varies, no backup if they leave |
| **Boutique Agency** | $1,500 - $5,000/mo | Industry knowledge, consistent quality | Less scalable |
| **Large Agency** | $3,000 - $10,000+/mo | Full resources, analytics, ad management | Expensive, less personal |
| **AI-Assisted (SuperSeller model)** | $500 - $1,500/mo | Scalable, consistent, data-driven | Needs human review for authenticity |

### What This Means for GP Homes Pricing

The sweet spot for a Plano contractor with zero social presence is **$500-$1,000/mo** for a starter package. This undercuts agencies ($1,500+) while delivering comparable value through AI-assisted content creation.

## 5. GP Homes — Specific Recommendations

### Starting Position
- **Instagram**: Non-existent. Need to create from scratch.
- **Facebook**: 23 likes. Essentially dead. Needs complete overhaul.
- **Google**: Has reviews (visible on Yelp with 33 photos). This is their strongest asset.
- **Website**: gphomesandrepairs.com exists and is functional.

### Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1)
1. Create Instagram Business account (@gphomesandrepairs)
2. Overhaul Facebook page with consistent branding
3. Set up link-in-bio (Linktree or custom) pointing to quote request
4. Harvest existing project photos from Yelp/website for initial content
5. Post 3x/week minimum to establish presence
6. Populate feed with 9-12 initial posts (grid looks full on first visit)

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 2-3)
1. Ramp to 5x/week Instagram, daily Stories, 2x/week Facebook
2. Start Instagram Reels with time-lapse and before/after content
3. Begin TikTok with repurposed Reels content
4. Run local hashtag strategy: #PlanoTX #DFWRemodeling #NorthTexasContractor #PlanoRemodeling
5. Cross-promote with Google reviews (ask happy customers to follow IG)

### Phase 3: Differentiation (Months 4-6)
1. AI-generated renovation mockups as lead magnets ("See what your kitchen COULD look like")
2. Video testimonials from customers
3. Neighborhood-specific content (Plano, Frisco, Allen, McKinney)
4. Partnership content with local material suppliers
