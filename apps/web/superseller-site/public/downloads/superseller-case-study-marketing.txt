# SuperSeller AI Case Study: Real Estate Video Marketing
## Result: 10x Faster Listing Videos at 1/10th the Cost

### Executive Summary
A real estate agent was spending $500+ per listing video and waiting 5-7 days for delivery. Using SuperSeller AI's **Forge** agent, they now generate cinematic property tour videos in minutes from a Zillow URL.

### Challenges
- $500+ per professional listing video
- 5-7 day turnaround per video
- Inconsistent quality across videographers
- No video for lower-priced listings (not worth the cost)

### The SuperSeller AI Solution
1. **Forge Video Agent**: Paste Zillow URL → AI generates cinematic property tour with realtor face compositing, room transitions, music, and text overlays
2. **Automatic Delivery**: Videos delivered via WhatsApp and email within minutes
3. **Universal Credits**: Same credits used for social content, call handling, and lead gen

### Results
- **Video Cost**: ~$15 per video (50 credits on Pro plan) vs $500+ traditional
- **Turnaround**: Minutes, not days
- **Coverage**: Every listing gets a video, not just premium ones
- **ROI**: System paid for itself with the first listing
