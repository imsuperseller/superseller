# SuperSeller AI — Business Automation Readiness Checklist

## Before You Start

1. **AI Crew Access**
   - [ ] SuperSeller AI account (superseller.agency)
   - [ ] Credit plan selected (Starter $79/mo, Pro $149/mo, or Team $299/mo)
   - [ ] Business URL ready for Forge video generation

2. **Video Production (Forge)**
   - [ ] Business photos/headshots available
   - [ ] Property listing URLs (for real estate)
   - [ ] Brand colors and logo for overlays

3. **Call Handling (FrontDesk)**
   - [ ] Dedicated phone number for AI receptionist
   - [ ] Business hours and routing preferences
   - [ ] FAQ and common questions documented

4. **Lead Generation (Scout)**
   - [ ] Target customer profile defined
   - [ ] Landing page content prepared
   - [ ] CRM or email for lead delivery

5. **Social Media (Buzz)**
   - [ ] Social accounts connected (FB, IG, LinkedIn, X, TikTok, YouTube)
   - [ ] Brand voice guidelines
   - [ ] Content calendar preferences

6. **Knowledge Base (Cortex)**
   - [ ] Business documents for ingestion (PDFs, docs, FAQs)
   - [ ] Team access requirements defined
