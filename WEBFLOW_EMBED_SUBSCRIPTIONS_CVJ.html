Frequently Asked Questions

How do you verify lead quality? +

All emails are verified using ZeroBounce (92%+ deliverability). Phone numbers are validated against carrier databases. We also filter out role-based emails (info@, sales@) and known spam traps.

Can I change my targeting criteria? +

Yes. You can update industries, locations, and filters monthly. Changes take effect in the next batch. Enterprise plans can adjust targeting anytime.

What if I don't use all my leads? +

Unused leads roll over for 90 days (Pro and Enterprise plans). Starter plan: use it or lose it monthly. You can also pause your subscription for up to 3 months.

Are these leads exclusive to me? +

Yes. Once delivered to you, those specific leads are never sold to anyone else. However, the same business may appear in multiple clients' lists if they target the same industry/location (just like any prospecting database).

How often are leads delivered? +

Starter: Monthly batch. Pro/Enterprise: Weekly or real-time via API. You choose your delivery schedule during onboarding.

What's your refund policy? +

14-day free trial (no credit card required). After that, if lead quality doesn't meet our 90%+ verification standard, we'll replace bad leads or issue a prorated refund. Cancel anytime—no contracts.

Can I get leads for multiple industries? +

Yes. Starter: 1 industry. Pro: 3 industries. Enterprise: Unlimited. You can split your monthly lead volume across industries however you want.
